2. App Description

The App is designed for land surveyors, geomatics engineers, and students in these fields. It allows users to:

  • Compute bearing and distance between coordinates.
  • Calculate area from coordinates and generate computation sheets.
  • Generate Bearing and Distance sheets, Plan Data sheets, and Beacon Index sheets.

All calculations are performed locally on your device, and no data is shared with external servers.
